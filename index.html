A terrarium is a tiny weather system. The biggest mistake is treating it like a normal pot.

Where to keep it?

Think bright, no direct sun. Keep it indoors, away from harsh sunlight.

How much watering?

  • Only use drinking water.
  • Use a spray bottle to avoid overwatering.
  • For open jars, spray lightly when soil looks dry or leaves seem limp.
  • For closed jars, you may not need to spray for months.
